Project Overview

项目概述

Program Overview

本项目专为柬埔寨中小学及大学学生设计，是一次走进中华文明发源地——古都西安的深度历史探索之旅。七天六夜，学员将踏入秦始皇兵马俑的恢宏军阵、登上中国保存最完整的明代城墙、亲历华清宫千年温泉与唐代盛世风华，感受十三朝古都的历史积淀，并体验古丝绸之路文化、唐代乐舞、中国陕菜烹饪等非遗技艺，开启一段跨越千年的文明对话。 This program is specially designed for Cambodian primary, secondary, and university students — a deep historical exploration of Xi'an, the ancient capital and cradle of Chinese civilization. Over 7 days and 6 nights, participants will stand before the Terracotta Warriors, walk the ancient Ming Dynasty city walls, experience Huaqing Palace's hot springs, and immerse in the grandeur of the Tang Dynasty's Silk Road culture, ancient dance, and Shaanxi culinary arts.

Day-by-Day Program Details

1
DAY 1 · 抵达长安，踏入千年古都 Arrival in Xi'an — Welcome to the Ancient Capital

初印象 · 盛唐气象 · First Impressions of the Thirteen-Dynasty Capital

全天
国际航班抵达西安咸阳国际机场 后勤 Logistics
International Flight Arrival at Xi'an Xianyang International Airport (XIY)

接机导师持牌迎接，专属大巴接送入住四星级酒店。发放中柬双语研学手册，辅导员进行西安城市文化简介——从周秦汉唐到今日古城。Welcoming reception by bilingual guides, transfer to 4-star hotel. Distribution of Khmer-Chinese bilingual study guides and Xi'an cultural briefing — from Zhou, Qin, Han & Tang dynasties to the modern ancient city.

15:00
大雁塔广场 · 大唐不夜城漫游 历史人文 History
Big Wild Goose Pagoda & Tang Paradise Night City

参观玄奘西行求法的大雁塔，了解佛教东传历史与唐代国际交流。傍晚漫步大唐不夜城，欣赏唐风建筑街区，感受盛唐文化复兴氛围。Visit the Big Wild Goose Pagoda built for Xuanzang's scriptures, learn about Buddhism's spread from India to China. Evening stroll through Tang Paradise Night City — a stunning recreation of Tang Dynasty street culture.

18:00
欢迎晚宴 · 开营仪式 文化 Culture
Welcome Dinner & Opening Ceremony

举行正式开营仪式，每位学员发放研学套装（毛笔+剪纸工具+纪念册）。品尝正宗陕西特色菜肴，导师介绍七天行程与学习目标，分组建队。Formal opening ceremony with study kit distribution (brush, paper-cutting tools & journal). Taste authentic Shaanxi cuisine. Schedule briefing, team introductions, and goal-setting session.

2
DAY 2 · 千古兵阵，世界第八奇迹 Terracotta Warriors — The Eighth Wonder of the World

秦代遗产 · 帝国意志 · Qin Dynasty Legacy & Imperial Power

09:00
秦始皇兵马俑博物馆深度参观 历史人文 History
Emperor Qin's Terracotta Army Museum — Deep Exploration

专业中英双语导游带领深度参观一、二、三号坑，近距离欣赏逾8,000件真人大小的陶俑与战马。了解秦朝统一六国历史、古代军事制度与陶艺工艺。约3小时深度游览。Expert bilingual tour through Pit 1, 2 & 3, viewing over 8,000 life-sized terracotta soldiers and horses. Learn about Qin Dynasty unification, ancient military systems, and pottery craftsmanship. ~3-hour deep exploration.

12:30
秦始皇陵封土堆参观 历史人文 History
Qin Shi Huang's Mausoleum — The Unexcavated Wonder

探访至今尚未发掘的秦始皇陵主陵封土堆，了解《史记》中记载的地下宫殿传说。完成研学任务卡"我眼中的始皇帝"，引导学生思考历史遗产保护与考古伦理。Visit the unexcavated main mausoleum mound, explore legends of the underground palace as recorded in the Shiji. Complete the "Emperor Qin in My Eyes" study card — reflecting on heritage preservation and archaeological ethics.

15:00
兵马俑彩绘陶俑DIY体验 艺术 Arts
Terracotta Warrior Painting Workshop

在非遗传承人指导下，亲手绘制仿真陶俑模型，了解古代彩绘工艺与矿物颜料使用方法。每位学员完成个人专属彩绘陶俑作品并带走。Under guidance of intangible heritage masters, paint your own terracotta warrior replica. Learn ancient pigment and coloring techniques. Each student takes home their own completed warrior.

3
DAY 3 · 城垣雄风，漫步千年古墙 The Ancient City Wall — Riding Along History

明代遗存 · 古城格局 · Ming Dynasty Wall & Historic Urban Grid

09:00
西安城墙骑行 · 俯瞰古城全景 体能挑战 Challenge
City Wall Cycling — A Panoramic Ride Through History

骑自行车沿中国保存最完整的明代城墙游览，全长13.74公里，感受古城壮阔格局。导师沿途讲解城墙建造历史、防御体系与各城门文化。Cycle along China's best-preserved Ming Dynasty city wall (13.74km circuit). Learn about construction history, defensive systems, and the cultural significance of each city gate from bilingual guides.

11:00
钟楼·鼓楼 · 晨钟暮鼓文化体验 历史人文 History
Bell Tower & Drum Tower — Morning Bell, Evening Drum

参观西安标志性钟楼与鼓楼，了解中国古代城市计时制度与礼乐文化。亲身体验敲鼓仪式，感受"晨钟暮鼓"的历史回响。Visit Xi'an's iconic Bell Tower and Drum Tower. Learn about ancient Chinese urban timekeeping and ritual music culture. Participate in the drum-striking ceremony and feel the resonance of history.

14:00
回民街 · 丝路美食文化之旅 文化 Culture
Muslim Quarter (Huimin Street) — Silk Road Food Culture

漫步西安最具异域风情的回民街，品尝羊肉泡馍、肉夹馍、凉皮等经典陕西小吃，感受千年丝绸之路带来的多元文化融合。了解西安回族历史与中阿文化交流。Stroll through Xi'an's vibrant Muslim Quarter, taste Yangrou Paomo, Roujiamo, and Liangpi. Experience the multicultural legacy of the Silk Road. Learn about Xi'an's Hui Muslim history and Arab-Chinese cultural exchange.

4
DAY 4 · 丝路非遗，千年技艺传承 Silk Road Intangible Heritage — Crafts Across Centuries

非物质遗产 · 文化创作 · Intangible Heritage & Artistic Immersion

09:00
陕西剪纸体验 · "我的生肖剪纸" 艺术 Arts
Shaanxi Paper-Cutting Workshop — "My Zodiac Cut"

国家级非遗剪纸传承人授课，学习剪纸基础刀法，完成以中国十二生肖为主题的个人剪纸作品。了解剪纸艺术的历史渊源与民俗意义，作品装框带走。National intangible heritage paper-cutting master teaches basic cutting techniques. Create a personalized Chinese zodiac paper-cut. Learn the folk history and symbolism behind this art form. Framed work to take home.

11:00
凤翔泥塑体验 · 手捏彩绘泥玩具 艺术 Arts
Fengxiang Clay Sculpture — Hand-Molded Color Folk Toys

体验陕西凤翔彩绘泥塑非遗工艺，亲手捏制并彩绘传统民俗造型（虎·鱼·福娃），感受"泥土变艺术"的创造乐趣，成品带走留念。Experience Fengxiang colored clay sculpting, a national intangible heritage craft. Hand-mold and paint traditional folk figures (tiger, fish, fortune doll). Take your completed sculpture home.

14:30
秦腔皮影戏欣赏 · 中国最古老的戏剧 文化 Culture
Qin Opera & Shadow Puppetry — China's Oldest Drama

欣赏国家级非遗秦腔折子戏，感受"百戏之祖"的磅礴气势。参与皮影戏互动体验，亲手操控皮影人物，了解中国皮影戏的历史起源与丝路传播之路。Watch a Qin Opera excerpt — China's oldest theatrical form and ancestor of all Chinese operas. Hands-on shadow puppetry experience: control characters yourself and learn about the Silk Road spread of this ancient art.

19:00
陕西厨艺课 · 手工擀面与肉夹馍制作 美食 Cuisine
Shaanxi Cooking Class — Hand-Rolled Noodles & Roujiamo

在当地厨师指导下，亲手学习制作陕西特色手工面条与肉夹馍（"中国汉堡"），了解陕菜饮食文化与小麦文明。亲手做、亲口吃，最有温度的文化课。Under local chefs' guidance, hand-roll Shaanxi noodles and assemble Roujiamo ("Chinese hamburger"). Learn about Shaanxi food culture and wheat civilization. Cook it, eat it — the most heartfelt cultural lesson.

5
DAY 5 · 盛唐华清，温泉与帝国的传奇 Tang Dynasty Glory — Huaqing Palace & Imperial Romance

唐代盛世 · 皇家温泉 · Tang Imperial Splendour & Royal Heritage

09:00
华清宫 · 皇家温泉与唐玄宗传奇 历史文化 Heritage
Huaqing Palace — Royal Hot Springs & Tang Emperor's Legend

游览千年皇家温泉胜地华清宫，参观莲花汤、海棠汤等唐代御用汤池遗址。了解唐玄宗与杨贵妃的历史传奇，感受白居易《长恨歌》所描绘的盛唐风华。探访西安事变旧址——五间厅。Tour the millennium-old royal hot spring resort. Visit Tang Dynasty imperial bathing pools. Learn about Emperor Xuanzong and Yang Guifei's legendary romance, immortalized in Bai Juyi's "Song of Everlasting Regret." Also visit the Xi'an Incident site.

13:00
大唐芙蓉园 · 唐代皇家园林体验 文化 Culture
Tang Paradise Garden — Imperial Garden Immersion

游览以唐代皇家御苑为原型复建的大唐芙蓉园，欣赏仿唐建筑群落，体验唐代诗词、歌舞与宫廷礼仪。观看大型实景演出《梦回大唐》，沉浸式感受盛唐文化。Explore the Tang Paradise Garden reconstructed after the Tang imperial garden. Admire Tang Dynasty architecture, participate in Tang poetry and court ritual experiences. Watch the spectacular live show "Dream of the Tang Dynasty."

16:00
中柬学生交流活动 · 丝路文化分享会 国际交流 Exchange
Cambodia-China Student Silk Road Cultural Exchange

与西安当地学生共同参与丝路主题文化交流活动，柬埔寨学生分享吴哥窟与高棉文明，中国学生介绍西安与丝绸之路，互赠文化礼品，探讨东西文明交汇的历史意义。Joint Silk Road cultural exchange with Xi'an students. Cambodian students share Angkor Wat and Khmer civilization; Chinese students introduce Xi'an and the Silk Road. Exchange gifts and discuss the historical significance of East-West civilization encounters.

6
DAY 6 · 自然人文，秦岭与历史博物馆 Nature & Heritage — Qinling Mountains & Museum of China

自然生态 · 文明脉络 · Natural Wonders & Civilization's Timeline

09:00
秦岭野生动物世界 · 大熊猫与金丝猴 生态 Ecology
Qinling Wildlife Park — Giant Panda & Golden Snub-Nosed Monkey

参访中国南北地理分界线——秦岭的野生动物保护基地，近距离观察大熊猫、国家一级保护动物金丝猴等珍稀物种。了解秦岭生物多样性与中国生态保护政策。Visit a wildlife conservation center at the Qinling Mountains — China's north-south geographical divide. Observe giant pandas, golden snub-nosed monkeys, and other rare species. Learn about Qinling biodiversity and China's conservation policies.

13:00
陕西历史博物馆 · 中国历史文化宝库 历史人文 History
Shaanxi History Museum — Treasure House of Chinese History

参观被誉为"中国第一博物馆"的陕西历史博物馆，馆藏逾170万件文物，涵盖从石器时代到明清的完整历史脉络。重点欣赏汉代金兽、唐代壁画、鎏金铜蚕等丝路珍宝。Visit Shaanxi History Museum — "China's First Museum" with over 1.7 million artifacts spanning the Stone Age to Qing Dynasty. Highlights: Han Dynasty Gold Beast, Tang murals, and gilded bronze silkworms — treasures of the Silk Road.

18:30
羊肉泡馍盛宴 · 告别晚宴 美食 Cuisine
Yangrou Paomo Feast — Farewell Dinner

在百年老字号享用西安最具代表性的羊肉泡馍，亲手掰馍体验传统饮食仪式。学员分享七天研学感悟，颁发"研学成就卡"，拍摄团体纪念照，共度难忘一夜。At a century-old Xi'an restaurant, enjoy the iconic Yangrou Paomo. Hand-break the bread yourself — a treasured local ritual. Students share 7-day reflections, receive "Achievement Cards," and take group commemorative photos.

7
DAY 7 · 凯旋启程，带走长安 Farewell Xi'an — Take the Ancient Capital Home

结营典礼 · 满载而归 · Closing Ceremony & Homebound Journey

09:00
碑林博物馆 · 中国书法艺术殿堂 历史文化 Heritage
Forest of Stone Steles Museum — Temple of Chinese Calligraphy

参观拥有逾千年历史的碑林博物馆，馆内珍藏历代石刻碑文逾11,000件。欣赏王羲之、颜真卿、柳公权等书法大家真迹拓本，感受汉字书法艺术的千年传承与文化魅力。Visit the millennium-old Forest of Stone Steles — China's premier calligraphy museum with over 11,000 carved stone tablets. Admire rubbings of masterworks by Wang Xizhi, Yan Zhenqing, and Liu Gongquan. Feel the thousand-year legacy of Chinese calligraphy.

11:00
结营典礼 · 结业证书颁发 结营 Closing
Closing Ceremony — Certificate Presentation

举行正式结营典礼，颁发"长安盛世探索营"双语结业证书、成绩手册及作品集。学员代表发表感言，播放七天影像回顾短片，留下最珍贵的记忆。Formal closing ceremony with presentation of bilingual completion certificates, achievement booklets, and portfolio collections. Student representative speeches and a 7-day video highlight reel — memories for a lifetime.

14:00
永兴坊 · 自由购物 & 出发 后勤 Logistics
Yongxing Fang — Free Shopping & Airport Transfer

前往永兴坊特色街区自由购物，选购陕西特产（秦腔面具、剪纸、柿饼等）及纪念品。专属大巴送往咸阳国际机场，乘坐国际航班返回金边。道别长安，满载而归。Free shopping at Yongxing Fang for Shaanxi specialties (Qin Opera masks, paper-cuts, persimmon cakes & more). Coach transfer to Xianyang airport for international flight back to Phnom Penh. Farewell Xi'an — homeward bound!
