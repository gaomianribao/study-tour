Project Overview

项目概述

Program Overview

本项目专为柬埔寨中小学及大学学生设计，是一次走进中国最独特的"魔幻山城"重庆的深度探索之旅。七天六夜，学员将登顶世界最大拱桥、探秘亚洲第一大瀑布群，近距离接触大熊猫与金丝猴，徒步"三生三世"绝美天生三桥，追溯红岩革命精神，登临大足石刻世界文化遗产，在洪崖洞灯火中感受中国西南最魔幻的城市夜景，开启一场集自然奇观、红色历史、世界遗产与赛博朋克于一体的立体研学体验。 This program is specially designed for Cambodian primary, secondary, and university students — a deep exploration of Chongqing, China's most unique "magic mountain city." Over 7 days and 6 nights, participants will explore the world's largest arch bridge, visit Asia's grandest waterfall cluster, see giant pandas and golden monkeys up close, trek the stunning Three Natural Bridges, trace Red Rock revolutionary history, visit the Dazu Rock Carvings UNESCO site, and experience the most surreal urban nightscape in Southwest China.

Day-by-Day Program Details

DAY 1 · 抵达山城，遇见魔幻之都 Arrival in Chongqing — Welcome to the Magic Mountain City

初印象 · 魔幻夜景 · First Impressions of China's Most Unique City

全天
国际航班抵达重庆江北国际机场 后勤 Logistics
International Flight Arrival at Chongqing Jiangbei International Airport (CKG)

接机导师持牌迎接，专属大巴送往四星级酒店。发放中柬双语研学手册，辅导员进行重庆城市文化简介——山城夜景、两江交汇、8D魔幻交通，让学员对这座"赛博朋克"城市充满期待。Welcoming reception by bilingual guides, transfer to 4-star hotel. Distribution of Khmer-Chinese bilingual study guides and Chongqing city briefing — mountain city nightscape, two-river confluence, and 8D magic transport — building excitement for this unique city.

18:00
洪崖洞 · 千与千寻魔幻夜景 文化 Culture
Hongya Cave — Spirited Away Night Scene

夜游洪崖洞景区——这里是宫崎骏《千与千寻》汤屋的现实原型。11层吊脚楼依山而建，灯火璀璨，两江（长江与嘉陵江）环抱，俯瞰重庆最震撼的夜景，感受"山城"与"江城"的双重魅力。Evening visit to Hongya Cave — the real-world inspiration for Miyazaki's "Spirited Away." This 11-story stilted building lights up magnificently between two rivers (Yangtze & Jialing), showcasing the dual magic of Chongqing as both a mountain city and a river city.

20:00
欢迎晚宴 · 开营仪式 文化 Culture
Welcome Dinner & Opening Ceremony

举行正式开营仪式，每位学员发放研学套装。品尝正宗重庆火锅（鸳鸯锅/微辣），体验"万物皆可烫"的重庆火锅文化。开营破冰游戏，分组建队，选出各组"山城探索官"。Formal opening ceremony with study kit distribution. Taste authentic Chongqing hotpot (half-half pot/mild spice). Experience the "everything can be dipped" hotpot culture. Ice-breaker games, team formation, and election of "Mountain City Explorers" for each group.

2
DAY 2 · 红色记忆，不朽的红岩精神 Red Rock Heritage — The Immortal Spirit of Revolutionary China

革命历史 · 爱国主义 · Revolutionary History & Patriotism

09:00
红岩革命纪念馆 · 革命历史深度研学 历史人文 History
Red Rock Revolutionary Memorial Museum — Deep Revolutionary Study

参观红岩革命纪念馆（抗日战争时期中共中央南方局驻地），学习革命先辈在重庆的斗争历史。通过珍贵文物、历史照片与多媒体展示，了解周恩来、董必武等老一辈革命家在此地的光辉岁月，完成研学任务卡"红岩精神与我"。Visit the Red Rock Revolutionary Memorial Museum (former Southern Bureau of the CPC Central Committee during the Anti-Japanese War). Learn about the revolutionary struggle through artifacts, photos, and multimedia. Complete the "Red Rock Spirit & Me" study card with guidance from bilingual guides.

11:30
歌乐山烈士陵园 · 缅怀革命先烈 历史人文 History
Gele Mountain Martyrs Cemetery — Honoring Revolutionary Heroes

参访歌乐山烈士陵园，向革命烈士敬献花圈，聆听渣滓洞、白公馆监狱历史，了解"11·27"大屠杀事件。引导学生思考和平来之不易，珍惜当下幸福生活，进行爱国主义主题课堂。Pay respects at the Martyrs Cemetery with flower wreath laying. Learn the history of Ji Zhi Gong and Bai Hao Gong prisons and the November 27 massacre. Patriotic theme class: reflecting on the value of peace and the importance of cherishing today's幸福生活.

15:00
解放碑 · 重庆城市地标徒步 文化 Culture
Jiefangbei · Chongqing City Landmark Walking Tour

徒步解放碑商圈——这里是重庆的心脏与地标，中国第一条商业步行街。导师讲解解放碑的历史（纪念抗日战争胜利）与今日繁华，感受重庆作为西南经济中心的蓬勃活力。Walking tour of Jiefangbei — Chongqing's heart and landmark, China's first commercial pedestrian street. Learn the history of Jiefangbei (memorial to victory in the Anti-Japanese War) and experience the bustling energy of Southwest China's economic center.

3
DAY 3 · 古镇悠悠，长江索道凌空飞渡 Ancient Town & Yangtze Cableway — Sky-High River Crossing

巴渝文化 · 历史街区 · Ba-Yu Culture & Historic Districts

09:00
磁器口古镇 · 巴渝民俗文化深度游 文化 Culture
Ciqikou Ancient Town — Ba-Yu Folk Culture Immersion

漫步千年古镇磁器口——重庆"最重庆"的历史街区。这里曾是嘉陵江畔重要的水运码头，如今保留了明清以来的吊脚楼、茶馆、川剧变脸等巴渝文化精髓。品尝陈麻花、毛血旺等重庆小吃，体验非遗茶馆文化与川剧变脸。Stroll through thousand-year-old Ciqikou — the "most Chongqing" historic district, once a key waterway port on the Jialing River. Taste Chen's麻花 and 毛血旺 snacks, experience the intangible heritage teahouse culture and Face-Changing Sichuan Opera.

14:00
长江索道 · "山城空中巴士"体验 体验 Experience
Yangtze River Cableway — "Mountain City's Sky Bus"

乘坐长江索道横跨长江——这是重庆独有的城市交通奇观，被誉为"山城空中巴士"。从车厢俯瞰两江四岸的壮丽景色，体验"山在城中，城在水中"的独特地貌，完成研学任务"山城立体交通图"。Ride the Yangtze River Cableway across the Yangtze — Chongqing's unique urban transport wonder. Enjoy panoramic views of both rivers and four banks from the cabin, experiencing the unique terrain where "mountains are in the city, cities are in the water." Complete the "Mountain City 3D Transport Map" study task.

18:30
南滨路 · 两江夜景主题课堂 文化 Culture
Nanbin Road — Two-River Night View Masterclass

沿南滨路（长江南岸）漫步，欣赏对岸渝中半岛的璀璨夜景——"字水霄灯"自古为巴渝十二景之一。导师现场开展"山城夜景文化"主题课，讲解重庆夜景形成的地理与气候原因（两江环抱、山水相依、雾气氤氲）。Stroll Nanbin Road (south bank of Yangtze), admiring the dazzling night view across the Yuzhong Peninsula — "Character Water Night Lights" has been one of Ba-Yu's 12 scenic spots since ancient times. On-site "Mountain City Nightscape Culture" masterclass with bilingual guides.

4
DAY 4 · 三峡博物馆，探秘鬼城传说 Three Gorges Museum & Legend of Ghost City

三峡文化 · 历史传说 · Three Gorges Culture & Historical Legends

09:00
重庆中国三峡博物馆 · 三峡文明通史 历史人文 History
China Three Gorges Museum — Complete History of Three Gorges Civilization

参观重庆中国三峡博物馆，系统学习长江三峡的地理形成、历史文化与三峡工程。通过4万余件文物，了解三峡地区从古人类到现代的完整文明脉络。重点学习三峡工程对文化遗产保护与移民安置的历史意义，完成研学任务"三峡工程利弊辩论"。Visit China Three Gorges Museum, systematically learning about the Yangtze Three Gorges' geography, history, and the Three Gorges Dam project. With over 40,000 artifacts, understand the complete civilization from ancient humans to modern times. Complete the "Three Gorges Dam: Pros & Cons Debate" study task.

13:30
丰都鬼城 · 中国传统鬼神文化探索 历史人文 History
Fengdu Ghost City — Traditional Chinese Spirit & Afterlife Culture

参访丰都鬼城——中国唯一以鬼文化为主题的景区，名山之上集道、佛、儒三教于一地。参观哼哈祠、鬼门关、黄泉路等景点，了解中国传统的生死观念与鬼神信仰，完成研学任务"中柬死亡文化对比"——探讨高棉文明与中华文明中对逝者的不同纪念方式。Visit Fengdu Ghost City — China's only ghost-culture themed attraction where Taoism, Buddhism, and Confucianism coexist. Tour Hengha Temple, Ghost Gate, and Yellow Springs Road. Complete the "Cambodian vs Chinese Death Culture Comparison" study task — exploring different ways Khmer and Chinese civilizations commemorate the deceased.

18:00
两江夜游 · 乘船穿越山城夜景 文化 Culture
Two-River Night Cruise — Sailing Through Mountain City Lights

乘船夜游长江与嘉陵江，从水上视角欣赏重庆夜景——渝中半岛层层叠叠的高楼、璀璨的洪崖洞、江北嘴的金融中心，交织成一幅赛博朋克风格的城市画卷。Night cruise on the Yangtze and Jialing Rivers. From the water, admire Chongqing's layered skyscrapers, glowing Hongya Cave, and Jiangbeizui financial center — a cyberpunk city painting unlike anywhere else in China.

5
DAY 5 · 天生三桥，地球最美的伤痕 Three Natural Bridges — Earth's Most Beautiful Scars

自然奇观 · 地质探秘 · Natural Wonders & Geological Exploration

07:30
武隆天生三桥 · 世界自然遗产深度探索 自然 Nature
Wulong Three Natural Bridges — UNESCO World Heritage Deep Exploration

前往世界自然遗产——武隆天生三桥景区。这里是电影《变形金刚4》和《满城尽带黄金甲》的取景地。三座自然形成的石拱桥（天龙桥、青龙桥、黑龙桥）横跨峡谷，气势磅礴。导师讲解喀斯特地貌成因，完成研学任务"天生三桥地质成因分析"。Visit the UNESCO World Heritage — Wulong Three Natural Bridges. Location for Transformers 4 and "Curse of the Golden Flower." Three natural stone arch bridges span the gorge spectacularly. Guides explain karst geology formation. Complete the "Geological Origins Analysis" study task.

12:30
仙女山国家森林公园 · 南方最美草原 自然 Nature
Fairy Mountain National Forest Park — South China's Most Beautiful Grassland

游览仙女山国家森林公园——海拔2,000米以上的"南方最美高山草原"。在辽阔草原上开展户外拓展活动（放风筝、草原团队竞技），体验"春看花、夏避暑、秋看叶、冬滑雪"的四季魅力，了解高山生态系统与草原环境保护知识。Tour Fairy Mountain National Forest Park — the "most beautiful highland grassland in South China" at 2,000+ meters altitude. Outdoor team activities on the vast grassland (kite-flying, team competitions). Learn about the alpine ecosystem and grassland environmental protection.

18:00
武隆特色农家晚宴 · 返程 美食 Cuisine
Wulong Farmhouse Dinner · Return to Chongqing

在武隆农家乐享用当地特色菜肴，体验重庆山区的乡村美食文化。餐后乘车返回重庆主城，途中回顾今日研学内容，完成研学日志。Local farmhouse cuisine experience in Wulong's rural area. After dinner, coach return to central Chongqing. Route review of today's study content and completion of study journals.

6
DAY 6 · 大足石刻，世界文化遗产与火锅传奇 Dazu Rock Carvings & Chongqing Hotpot — World Heritage & Culinary Legend

世界遗产 · 巴渝美食 · World Heritage & Ba-Yu Cuisine

09:00
大足石刻 · 世界文化遗产深度研学 历史人文 History
Dazu Rock Carvings — UNESCO World Heritage Deep Study

参访世界文化遗产——大足石刻（宝顶山与北山两处）。这里是中国晚期石窟艺术的巅峰之作，5万余尊造像以佛教为主，融合儒、道两家，展示了中华文明的包容与融合。导师深度讲解"卧佛"与"千手观音"等经典造像，引导学生理解"慈悲济世"的普世价值，完成研学任务"大足石刻艺术风格分析"。Visit UNESCO World Heritage — Dazu Rock Carvings (Baoding Mountain & Beishan). Peak late-period Chinese grotto art with 50,000+ statues. Buddhist-dominant with Confucian and Taoist elements. Deep analysis of the "Reclining Buddha" and "Thousand-Hand Guanyin." Complete the "Dazu Art Style Analysis" study task.

14:00
重庆火锅博物馆 · 火锅文化深度体验 美食 Cuisine
Chongqing Hotpot Museum — Deep Hotpot Culture Experience

参访重庆火锅博物馆，了解火锅从清代江边码头纤夫饮食到风靡全球的美食传奇的全过程。在专业厨师指导下，亲手体验"打锅底"（炒制火锅底料），认识花椒、辣椒等巴渝特色香料，颁发"小小火锅传承人"证书。Visit Chongqing Hotpot Museum to trace the journey from Qing Dynasty dock workers' food to global culinary phenomenon. Under professional chefs' guidance, hands-on "base sauce making" experience. Learn about Sichuan peppercorns and chilies. Receive the "Little Hotpot Inheritor" certificate.

17:00
柬中青少年文化交流 · 告别晚宴 国际交流 Exchange
Cambodia-China Youth Cultural Exchange & Farewell Dinner

与重庆本地学生共同参与中柬青少年文化交流活动，柬埔寨学生分享吴哥窟文明与高棉文化，双方共同展示才艺，交换文化礼物，探讨"一带一路"背景下柬中青年合作的新机遇。告别晚宴品鉴重庆老火锅，学员分享六天研学感悟。Joint youth cultural exchange with Chongqing students. Cambodian students share Angkor civilization and Khmer culture; both sides perform talents and exchange cultural gifts. Discuss new Cambodia-China youth cooperation opportunities under the Belt and Road Initiative. Farewell dinner featuring authentic old-method Chongqing hotpot with student reflections.

DAY 7 · 凯旋启程，带走山城 Farewell Chongqing — Take the Mountain City Home

结营典礼 · 满载而归 · Closing Ceremony & Homebound Journey

09:30
结营典礼 · 研学成果展示 结营 Closing
Closing Ceremony — Study Tour Achievement Showcase

举行正式结营典礼，各小组展示研学成果（红岩精神报告、大足石刻艺术分析、天生三桥地质报告等）。颁发"山城雾都探秘营"双语结业证书、成绩手册及研学作品集。学员代表发表感言，播放七天影像回顾短片，留下最珍贵的记忆。Formal closing ceremony with group study tour achievement presentations (Red Rock Spirit Reports, Dazu Art Analysis, Natural Bridges Geology Reports). Bilingual completion certificates, achievement booklets, and portfolio collections. Student representative speeches and 7-day video highlight reel — memories for a lifetime.

11:30
重庆动物园 · 野生国宝大熊猫 生态 Ecology
Chongqing Zoo — Wild Giant Pandas of China

参访重庆动物园，这里是中国大熊猫种群重要的科研与展示基地之一。观赏大熊猫进食与嬉戏，学习大熊猫保护知识与中国的生物多样性保护成就，完成研学任务"大熊猫与高棉象——中柬濒危物种保护对比"。Visit Chongqing Zoo, one of China's key giant panda research and exhibition bases. Observe pandas feeding and playing. Learn about panda conservation and China's biodiversity achievements. Complete the "Giant Panda vs Khmer Elephant — Cambodia-China Endangered Species Protection Comparison" study task.

14:30
返程出发 · 永别山城 后勤 Logistics
Airport Transfer & Farewell Mountain City

专属大巴送往江北国际机场，乘坐国际航班返回金边。道别重庆，满载七天研学收获而归。期待下次再会这座"永远到达不了的城市"——山城重庆。Dedicated coach to Jiangbei International Airport for international flight back to Phnom Penh. Farewell Chongqing — homeward bound with seven days of study tour treasures. See you next time in this "city that can never be reached" — Chongqing, the Mountain City!
