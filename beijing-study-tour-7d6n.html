Project Overview

项目概述

Program Overview

本项目专为柬埔寨中小学及大学学生设计，是一次走进中华文明核心——北京的深度文化探索之旅。七天六夜，学员将穿行于故宫宏大宫廷、登临万里长城、漫步颐和园皇家御苑，深入了解中国五千年历史文明，并体验传统书法、水墨画、中国功夫等文化精髓，同时走进高科技园区与顶尖高校，感受中国现代创新力量。 This program is specially designed for Cambodian primary, secondary, and university students — a deep cultural discovery journey to Beijing, the heart of Chinese civilization. Over 7 days and 6 nights, participants will explore the Forbidden City, climb the Great Wall, stroll through the Summer Palace, and experience traditional calligraphy, ink painting, martial arts, and modern innovation hubs.

详细行程安排

Day-by-Day Program Details

1
DAY 1 · 抵达京城，开启华章 Arrival in Beijing — The Journey Begins

初印象 · 皇城气度 · First Impressions of the Imperial Capital

全天
国际航班抵达北京首都国际机场 后勤 Logistics
International Flight Arrival at Beijing Capital International Airport (PEK)

接机导师持牌迎接，专属大巴接送入住四星级酒店。发放中柬双语研学手册，辅导员进行北京城市文化简介。Welcoming reception by bilingual guides, transfer to 4-star hotel. Distribution of Khmer-Chinese bilingual study guides and Beijing cultural briefing.

15:00
天安门广场 · 国旗广场参观 历史人文 History
Tiananmen Square — The Heart of China

参观天安门广场，了解广场历史文化意义，近距离感受新中国的标志性建筑。可观赏人民英雄纪念碑、人民大会堂外观。Visit Tiananmen Square, learn about its historical and cultural significance, view the Monument to the People's Heroes and the Great Hall of the People.

18:00
欢迎晚宴 · 开营仪式 文化 Culture
Welcome Dinner & Opening Ceremony

举行正式开营仪式，每位学员发放研学套装（笔墨纸砚+纪念册）。导师介绍七天行程与学习目标，分组建队，互相认识。Formal opening ceremony, distribution of study kits (calligraphy set & journal). Team introductions, schedule briefing, and goal-setting session.

2
DAY 2 · 紫禁盛世，千年皇城 Forbidden City — Ten Thousand Rooms, One Dynasty

历史印记 · 皇城文化 · Imperial Heritage & Palace Culture

09:00
故宫博物院深度参观 历史人文 History
Forbidden City (Palace Museum) — Deep Exploration

专业中英双语导游带领深度参观，游览午门、太和殿、乾清宫、御花园等核心区域。了解明清两代皇室生活与宫廷文化，时长约3小时。Bilingual expert guide tour through Meridian Gate, Hall of Supreme Harmony, Palace of Heavenly Purity, Imperial Garden. ~3-hour deep exploration of Ming & Qing dynasty imperial life.

12:30
景山公园 · 俯瞰紫禁城全景 历史人文 History
Jingshan Park — Bird's Eye View of the Forbidden City

登上景山万春亭，俯瞰故宫全景，感受皇城规制之宏大。完成研学任务卡"我眼中的北京中轴线"。Climb to Wanchun Pavilion for a panoramic view of the Forbidden City. Complete the "Beijing Central Axis in My Eyes" study mission card.

15:00
南锣鼓巷胡同文化体验 文化 Culture
Nanluoguxiang Hutong — Old Beijing Alley Culture

漫步老北京胡同，探访四合院，品尝冰糖葫芦、老北京小吃。了解北京平民生活文化，与当地居民互动交流。Stroll through historic hutongs, visit traditional courtyard houses, taste candied hawthorn skewers and Beijing snacks. Interact with local residents about daily life culture.

3
DAY 3 · 登临天堑，不到长城非好汉 The Great Wall — A Hero's Summit

奇迹遗产 · 意志磨砺 · World Heritage & Personal Challenge

08:00
出发慕田峪长城 后勤 Logistics
Departure to Mutianyu Great Wall

专属大巴出发，约1.5小时车程抵达慕田峪长城景区。沿途导师讲解长城建造历史与军事意义。Dedicated coach to Mutianyu Great Wall (~1.5 hours). En-route commentary on Great Wall construction history and military significance.

10:00
长城徒步登顶 · 颁发"好汉证书" 体能挑战 Challenge
Great Wall Hike & "Hero Certificate" Ceremony

分组徒步攀登长城，感受烽火台与城垛的历史温度。登顶后举行简短颁证仪式，每位学员获得"长城好汉"纪念证书。Group hike along the wall, experience watchtowers and battlements. Certificate ceremony at the summit — every participant earns a "Great Wall Hero" commemorative certificate.

14:00
明十三陵 · 帝王陵寝探访 历史人文 History
Ming Tombs — Mausoleum of the Emperors

参观明代皇帝陵寝，了解中国古代陵寝建筑艺术与丧葬文化。探访神道石像生，感受皇权象征的宏大气势。Visit the Ming Dynasty imperial mausoleums, learn about ancient Chinese funerary architecture. Explore the Sacred Way with its stone animal and official sculptures.

4
DAY 4 · 传统艺术，千年技艺 Traditional Arts — A Thousand Years of Mastery

非遗技艺 · 文化创作 · Intangible Heritage & Artistic Creation

09:00
书法体验课 · "写我的中文名" 艺术 Arts
Chinese Calligraphy Workshop — "Write My Chinese Name"

专业书法教师授课，学习毛笔使用方法，练习"福""寿""和"等吉祥汉字。每位学员将自己的柬文名字转为中文，完成个人专属书法作品并装裱带走。Professional calligrapher teaches brush technique, practicing auspicious characters. Each student translates their Khmer name to Chinese characters and creates a personalized framed calligraphy piece to take home.

11:00
水墨画体验 · 山水与花鸟 艺术 Arts
Chinese Ink Painting Workshop — Landscapes & Birds

学习中国传统水墨画基础技法，体验山水与花鸟两种经典题材，完成个人水墨画作品一幅。感受"留白之美"与东方美学。Learn basic traditional Chinese ink painting techniques in landscape and bird-flower genres. Complete one personal ink painting while experiencing the beauty of negative space and Eastern aesthetics.

14:30
中国功夫体验课 · 太极 & 少林拳 体育 Sports
Chinese Martial Arts — Tai Chi & Shaolin Basics

武术教练现场教学太极基础动作与少林基本拳，体验中国武术的"内外兼修"哲学。学员互相对练，拍摄纪念视频。Martial arts instructors teach Tai Chi basics and Shaolin fundamentals. Experience the "mind-body unity" philosophy of Chinese martial arts. Group practice and commemorative video recording.

19:00
京剧脸谱DIY · 传统戏曲欣赏 文化 Culture
Peking Opera Mask Painting & Performance Appreciation

自己动手绘制京剧脸谱，了解不同颜色代表的人物性格。观赏15分钟京剧经典折子戏，感受中国戏曲艺术之美。Paint your own Peking Opera mask and learn the symbolism of different colors. Watch a 15-minute classical Peking Opera excerpt and appreciate the beauty of Chinese theatrical arts.

5
DAY 5 · 科技未来，仰望星辰 Technology & Future — Reaching for the Stars

科创探索 · 名校研访 · Innovation & Elite University Experience

09:00
中国科技馆 · 互动科学体验 科技 Technology
China Science & Technology Museum — Interactive Learning

参观中国国家级科技博物馆，体验航空航天、人工智能、机器人等前沿科技展区。完成STEM研学任务卡，激发科学思维。Visit China's national science museum. Experience cutting-edge exhibits in aerospace, AI, and robotics. Complete STEM study mission cards to stimulate scientific thinking.

13:00
清华大学 / 北京大学校园参访 科创 Innovation
Campus Visit — Tsinghua University / Peking University

参访中国顶尖高校，漫步校园，了解中国高等教育体系与留学政策。与在读中国大学生进行交流座谈，激发学术志向。Visit China's top universities, tour the campus, and learn about higher education and international admission. Exchange session with Chinese university students to inspire academic aspirations.

16:00
中柬学生交流活动 · 文化分享会 国际交流 Exchange
Cambodia-China Student Cultural Exchange Forum

与北京当地学生共同参与文化交流活动，柬埔寨学生展示本国文化，中国学生介绍北京传统，互赠文化礼品，建立跨国友谊。Joint cultural exchange with Beijing students. Cambodian students showcase their culture, Chinese students share Beijing traditions. Exchange cultural gifts and build international friendships.

6
DAY 6 · 自然人文，皇家御苑 Nature & Heritage — The Imperial Garden

自然生态 · 皇家文化 · Royal Garden & Natural Education

09:00
颐和园皇家园林深度游 历史文化 Heritage
Summer Palace — Imperial Garden Exploration

参观世界文化遗产颐和园，游览昆明湖、长廊、佛香阁等核心景观。了解清代皇家园林艺术与造园哲学，体验"天人合一"的东方美学。Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Summer Palace: Kunming Lake, Long Corridor, Tower of Buddhist Incense. Understand Qing dynasty royal garden aesthetics and the philosophy of "harmony between man and nature."

12:00
北京动物园 · 大熊猫自然教育 生态 Ecology
Beijing Zoo — Giant Panda Nature Education

近距离观察国宝大熊猫，参与专业讲解员带领的大熊猫保护知识课程。了解濒危物种保护与生态多样性，完成"熊猫观察记录卡"。Observe giant pandas up close, participate in expert-led conservation education. Learn about endangered species protection and biodiversity. Complete "Panda Observation Record Card."

18:30
正宗北京烤鸭盛宴 · 告别晚宴 美食 Cuisine
Authentic Peking Duck Feast — Farewell Dinner

前往百年老字号享用正宗北京烤鸭，厨师现场片鸭展示。学员分享七天研学感悟，颁发"研学成就卡"，拍摄团体纪念照。Visit a century-old restaurant for authentic Peking Duck. Watch the chef carve the duck tableside. Students share their 7-day reflections, receive "Achievement Cards," and take group commemorative photos.

7
DAY 7 · 凯旋启程，带走北京 Farewell Beijing — Take the World Home

结营典礼 · 满载而归 · Closing Ceremony & Homebound Journey

09:00
天坛公园 · 天地礼制探访 历史文化 Heritage
Temple of Heaven — Ritual and Cosmology

参观天坛祈年殿，了解中国古代天地祭祀礼仪文化。感受中轴线建筑的对称之美与宇宙哲学。在天坛公园体验晨练文化，观看当地市民打太极、下象棋。Visit the Temple of Heaven's Hall of Prayer, learn about ancient Chinese sky-worship rituals. Witness local morning exercise culture — citizens practicing Tai Chi and playing Chinese chess.

11:00
结营典礼 · 结业证书颁发 结营 Closing
Closing Ceremony — Certificate Presentation

举行正式结营典礼，颁发"帝都华章探索营"双语结业证书、成绩手册及作品集。学员代表发表感言，播放七天影像回顾短片。Formal closing ceremony with presentation of bilingual completion certificates, achievement booklets, and portfolio collections. Student representative speeches and a 7-day video highlight reel.

14:00
王府井大街 · 自由购物 & 出发 后勤 Logistics
Wangfujing Street — Free Shopping & Airport Transfer

前往王府井步行街自由购物，选购北京特产及纪念品。专属大巴送往机场，乘坐国际航班返回金边。道别北京，满载而归。Free shopping time at Wangfujing pedestrian street for Beijing specialties and souvenirs. Coach transfer to airport for international flight back to Phnom Penh. Farewell Beijing — homeward bound!
