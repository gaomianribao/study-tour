Project Overview

项目概述

Program Overview

本项目专为柬埔寨中小学及大学学生设计，是一次走进中华文明核心——北京的深度文化探索之旅。七天六夜，学员将穿行于故宫宏大宫廷、登临万里长城、漫步颐和园皇家御苑，深入了解中国五千年历史文明，并体验传统书法、水墨画、中国功夫等文化精髓，同时走进高科技园区与顶尖高校，感受中国现代创新力量。 This program is specially designed for Cambodian primary, secondary, and university students — a deep cultural discovery journey to Beijing, the heart of Chinese civilization. Over 7 days and 6 nights, participants will explore the Forbidden City, climb the Great Wall, stroll through the Summer Palace, and experience traditional calligraphy, ink painting, martial arts, and modern innovation hubs.

初见北京 · 开营破冰 · First Arrival · Opening Ceremony

抵达北京，接机入住 后勤 Logistics
Arrival in Beijing — Airport Pickup & Check-in

专车接机，前往四星级酒店办理入住，发放研学资料包与营服。辅导员进行北京城市介绍，帮助学员熟悉环境。Airport pickup by dedicated coach, check-in at 4-star hotel. Distribution of study materials and camp uniforms. Welcome briefing on Beijing city culture.

18:00
欢迎晚宴 · 开营仪式 文化 Culture
Welcome Dinner & Opening Ceremony

正式举行开营仪式，介绍七天行程安排与学习目标，分组建队，领取研学手册与纪念品。品尝北京特色美食。Formal opening ceremony, schedule briefing, team formation, and distribution of study materials. Enjoy authentic Beijing cuisine.

DAY 2 · 皇城探秘，天安门 · 国博 · 故宫 Tiananmen · National Museum · Forbidden City

历史人文 · 皇城文化 · Imperial Heritage & National Museum

08:00
天安门广场 · 人民英雄纪念碑 历史人文 History
Tiananmen Square — Monument to the People's Heroes

参观天安门广场，瞻仰人民英雄纪念碑外观，了解其背后的历史故事，感受共和国心脏的庄严氛围。Visit Tiananmen Square, view the Monument to the People's Heroes, learn its historical significance and the solemn atmosphere of China's heart.

10:00
中国国家博物馆深度参观 历史人文 History
National Museum of China — Deep Exploration

参观中国国家博物馆，穿越五千年中华文明史。重点参观古代中国展馆、青铜器展馆，了解华夏文明发展脉络与重要历史文物。Visit the National Museum of China, tracing 5,000 years of Chinese civilization. Focus on the Ancient China and Bronze artifacts exhibitions.

14:00
故宫博物院深度参观 历史人文 History
Forbidden City (Palace Museum) — Deep Exploration

专业导游带领深度游览故宫，探访午门、太和殿、乾清宫、御花园等核心区域，讲述明清皇室历史与宫廷文化，完成研学任务卡。Bilingual expert guide through Meridian Gate, Hall of Supreme Harmony, Palace of Heavenly Purity. Complete study mission cards on imperial history.

3
DAY 3 · 登长城 · 鸟巢水立方 · 杂技 Great Wall · Bird's Nest · Acrobatic Show

奇迹遗产 · 现代地标 · World Heritage & Beijing Landmarks

07:30
前往慕田峪长城 后勤 Logistics
Transfer to Mutianyu Great Wall

乘坐专属大巴前往慕田峪长城（约1.5小时车程），导师介绍长城建造历史与军事意义。Dedicated coach to Mutianyu Great Wall (~1.5 hours). En-route briefing on Great Wall history and military significance.

09:00
慕田峪长城徒步 · 颁发"好汉证书" 体能挑战 Challenge
Mutianyu Great Wall Hike & "Hero Certificate"

分组攀登慕田峪长城，感受烽火台与城垛的历史厚重，登顶后颁发"长城好汉"纪念证书。Group hike along Mutianyu Great Wall. Certificate ceremony at the summit — "Great Wall Hero" commemorative certificate for every participant.

14:00
鸟巢 · 水立方外观游览 现代地标 Modern Landmark
Bird's Nest & Water Cube — Exterior Tour

外观参观北京奥运会主场馆鸟巢与水立方，导师讲解奥运历史与北京2008年奥运故事，在国家体育场前合影留念。Exterior visit of Beijing Olympic main stadium (Bird's Nest) and Water Cube. Learn about Olympic history and 2008 Beijing Olympics, group photo at National Stadium.

19:30
北京杂技观赏 艺术 Arts
Beijing Acrobatics Show

观赏国家级杂技表演，体验中国传统杂技艺术的惊险与美感，近距离感受力与美的完美结合。Watch a national-level acrobatics show, experiencing the thrill and beauty of traditional Chinese acrobatics. Witness the perfect combination of strength and grace.

4
DAY 4 · 索尼探梦 · 颐和园 · 昆明湖游船 Sony Explora · Summer Palace · Boat Ride

科技体验 · 皇家园林 · Science & Imperial Garden

09:00
索尼探梦科技馆体验 科技 Technology
Sony Explora Science Museum

参观索尼探梦科技馆，体验声光电磁等趣味科学实验，参与互动展项，激发科学兴趣与创新思维。Explore Sony Explora Science Museum. Hands-on exhibits in acoustics, optics, magnetism and more. Interactive experiments to spark scientific interest and creative thinking.

13:00
颐和园深度游览 历史人文 History
Summer Palace — Imperial Garden Exploration

游览世界文化遗产颐和园，漫步昆明湖畔，参观长廊、佛香阁，感受清代皇家园林的宏大与精巧。Explore UNESCO World Heritage Summer Palace. Stroll along Kunming Lake, visit the Long Corridor and Tower of Buddhist Incense. Experience the grandeur of Qing dynasty imperial gardens.

15:00
昆明湖游船体验 文化 Culture
Kunming Lake Boat Ride

乘坐画舫游览昆明湖，从湖面视角欣赏十七孔桥、西堤等经典景观，感受皇家园林的水墨意境。Scenic boat ride on Kunming Lake. Enjoy views of the Seventeen-Arch Bridge and West Causeway from the water. Experience the ink-wash landscape beauty of the imperial garden.

5
DAY 5 · 名校拜访 · 非遗手工课 University Visit & Intangible Heritage Crafts

名校研访 · 文化体验 · Elite Campus & Cultural Crafts

08:30
名校拜访 · 清华/北大校园参访 科创 Innovation
Campus Visit — Tsinghua University / Peking University

参访中国顶尖高校清华或北大，漫步校园，参访校史馆或博物馆，与在校师生交流座谈，了解中国高等教育体系与留学政策。Visit top Chinese universities (Tsinghua or Peking), campus tour, university history museum, exchange session with students and faculty. Learn about higher education and international admission policies.

13:30
非遗手工体验课 艺术 Arts
Intangible Cultural Heritage Craft Workshop

参与非遗手工体验课程，可选项目包括：京剧脸谱绘制、中国结艺、剪纸、泥塑等传统手工艺，完成个人作品可带走作为珍贵纪念。Participate in intangible cultural heritage craft workshops: Peking Opera mask painting, Chinese knotting, paper-cutting, clay sculpture. Complete a personal artwork to take home as a cherished souvenir.

18:00
北京烤鸭特色晚宴 美食 Cuisine
Authentic Peking Duck Dinner

前往老字号品尝正宗北京烤鸭，厨师现场片鸭展示，感受中华美食文化魅力。Visit a time-honored restaurant for authentic Peking Duck. Watch the chef carve the duck tableside. Experience the charm of Chinese culinary culture.

乘坐专属大巴前往北京环球影城主题公园，开启全天欢乐畅玩之旅。Dedicated coach transfer to Universal Studios Beijing for a full day of fun and adventure.

09:00–20:00
北京环球影城全天自由畅玩 欢乐体验 Fun
Universal Studios Beijing — All-Day Adventure

全天自由体验环球影城各大主题园区，包括哈利·波特的魔法世界、变形金刚基地、侏罗纪世界努布拉岛、好莱坞大道等。完成研学任务卡：记录最喜欢的项目并说明理由。Full-day exploration of Universal Studios Beijing's themed zones: Harry Potter, Transformers, Jurassic World, Hollywood Boulevard, and more. Complete study card: note your favorite attraction and explain why.
